4.8.1.2.2 Annotation 3D Geometry

The 'Annotation 3D Geometry' is used, when the representation of the annotation does includes surfaces and is not restricted to two-dimensional representation items.

The following attribute values for the IfcShapeRepresentation holding this geometric representation shall be used:

  • IfcShapeRepresentation.RepresentationIdentifier : 'Annotation'
  • IfcShapeRepresentation.RepresentationType : 'GeometricSet'
  • IfcShapeRepresentation.Items :
    • subtypes of IfcPoint and IfcCurve being three-dimensional and within an IfcGeometricSet
    • subtypes of IfcSurface being three-dimensional and within an IfcGeometricSet
